Privacy Policy

Effective date: [ADD DATE]

This Privacy Policy explains how Peters Construction (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the assetRACK mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and authentication details needed to create and access your account.
  • Organization and inventory data you provide: company name, invite codes, locations/zones, item names, notes, and related inventory records you enter.
  • Usage and diagnostics: basic app diagnostics and crash information may be collected to improve stability and performance.

How we use information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your organization’s workspace.
  • To store and display your inventory, locations, and item records.
  • To maintain, protect, and improve the Service (including debugging and performance monitoring).
  • To respond to support requests and service-related communications.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these situations:

  • Service providers: We use third-party services to operate the Service (for example, authentication and database hosting). These providers process data on our behalf.
  • Within your organization: Your inventory and organization data may be visible to other authorized users in the same workspace.
  • Legal and safety: If required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Third-party services

The Service may use third-party infrastructure (for example Firebase) for authentication and data storage. Their processing of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain account and organization data for as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion by contacting us (see Contact section below).

Your choices

  • Correction: You can edit many inventory fields directly in the app.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date when changes are made.
